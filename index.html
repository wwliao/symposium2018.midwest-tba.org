About Us

The goal of MTBA is to provide a platform for professionals in biotechnology to share information, educate, promote dialogue, and provide social activities.

The Midwest Taiwanese Biotechnology Association (MTBA) is a professional organization initiated in the summer of 2017 by a group of young bio-scientists across cities in the Midwest United States, including Ann Arbor (MI), Chicago (IL), Columbus (OH), Kansas City (MO), Madison (WI), St. Louis (MO), Indianapolis and West Lafayette (IN). The concept was first raised by the St. Louis chapter of North American Taiwanese Engineering & Science Association (NATEA), and later elaborated to a stand-alone regional organization. The goal of MTBA is to provide a platform for professionals in biotechnology to share information, educate, promote dialogue, and provide social activities. The first mission of MTBA is to hold the first Midwest Taiwanese Biotechnology Symposium in Chicago in the fall of 2018.
MTBA is looking for active members who would like to join and organize events, including symposia, webinars, and regional meetings. Please contact us for more information.
